Enchantment and Revelry

WHO WE ARE

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the enchantment of the Grand Rapids Renaissance Fair, where history springs to life amidst the natural beauty of Michigan's heartland. Since 1991, our faigrounds have transformed into a land of whimsical knights, jesters, minstrels, and artisans. Experiance the thrills of jousing tournamnets, the beauty of period musicians, and the artistry of skilled crafters from all corners of the realm. Our faire is a celebration of historical discovery, interactive theater, and communal joy, inviting one and all to partake in the pageantry and revelry that have become the hallmark of the Grand Rapids Renaissance Fair.

Annie and Julie, sisters and stewards of tradition, bring the Grand Rapids Renaissance Fair to life. Annie's mastery of historical detail transforms our marketplace into a true medieval tapestry, while Julie's lilting ballads echo the joy of bygone eras. Together, they craft a fair brimming with warmth and the vibrant spirit of the Renaissance.

King Arthur and Queen Guinevere grace the Grand Rapids Renaissance Fair with regal charm and benevolent rule. Their majestic presence at the court, adorned with opulent garments and crowns, inspires a realm of chivalry and splendor, inviting all to step into a storybook world of noble pageantry.

Jenny, the fair's mirthful jester, dances through the Grand Rapids Renaissance Fair with a sparkle that captivates the young and old alike. Her playful antics and quick-witted banter are a treasured spectacle, bringing laughter and light-heartedness to every corner of the festival.
