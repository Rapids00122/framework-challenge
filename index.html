Enchantment and Revelry

WHO WE ARE

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the enchantment of the Grand Rapids Renaissance Fair, where history springs to life amidst the natural beauty of Michigan's heartland. Since 1991, our faigrounds have transformed into a land of whimsical knights, jesters, minstrels, and artisans. Experiance the thrills of jousing tournamnets, the beauty of period musicians, and the artistry of skilled crafters from all corners of the realm. Our faire is a celebration of historical discovery, interactive theater, and communal joy, inviting one and all to partake in the pageantry and revelry that have become the hallmark of the Grand Rapids Renaissance Fair.
